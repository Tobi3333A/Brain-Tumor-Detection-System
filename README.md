# Brain Tumor Detection System
This is a machine learning model that can tell, wth up to 97% accuracy, whether a brain has tumor from an MRI scan of the brain.

## Overview
With this model, people can tell whether they have a brain tumor and trust that they result they get is most likely accurate because out of 1222 MRI scans tested th this model, only 8 predictions were wrong.

![Sample Test Result Image](/result-image.png)
This image shows the system predicting whether those MRI scans show brains with tumor or no tumor. Our dataset shows that the first three (first row) images do not have brain tumor and the second three (second row) have brain tumor; however, the MRI scan in the middle of the second row shows "no tumor" as this is one of the eight case the system failed to get right.
